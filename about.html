About

Mission

Women Techmakers is a global program created by Google to highlight the talent of women in technology. Together with other sponsors and partners, Google is supporting the development of women in technology.

Women Techmakers Hamburg is independent from Google and our other partners. The events and speakers that we selected are based our ideas and knowledge. This community promotes talented and passionate women and increases the visibility of the Hamburg tech community while honoring women in technology. We want to provide visibility, community and resources for women interested in tech.

Values

Respect, trust, fairness, embrace differences, sharing and supporting, improvement.

Our values are what we think everyone should be based on. They are also what we use to benchmark our actions. We want everyone respected as a person, having them trusted, treated in a fair manner.

We want to have equality in value while understanding the differences — we think that using different mindsets together delivers way better quality. We want information to be shared and colleagues to be supported, all for the purpose of continuous improvement.
